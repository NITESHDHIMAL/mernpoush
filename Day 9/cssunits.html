Nepal’s IT and ICT sector importent is steadily growing as a contributor to the national economy. In 2022, ICT services (including IT outsourcing, software, and BPO) accounted for ≈1.7% of Nepal’s nominal GDP and added roughly +0.3 percentage points to annual real GDP growth between 2012 and 2024. The sector’s export performance is notable: IT service exports reached USD 515 million (≈ NPR 67.5 billion) in 2022, representing ≈1.4% of GDP and contributing about 5.5% of Nepal’s foreign exchange reserves. Over the past two decades, digitally delivered services have grown at a CAGR of ~11.6%, and by 2023, they accounted for over 50% of Nepal’s total services exports. (source)
